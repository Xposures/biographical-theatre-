The power of storytelling: Write down your two stories based on the two questions that you have chosen in the gallery walk?

Task 3

How would your younger self feel about your present self?


My younger self is proud of what we have become. Especially that we are so much more confident today than we ever were as a kid. Also my younger self is pleasantly surprised that Mika and I are still friends and that so much more great friends are supporting us and chose to walk a part of their way alongside ours. Younger me is also very proud of the healthy way we deal with problems these days.
The younger version of myself likes the present version of myself very much and is convinced that there is a lot of good coming our way.


What is your imagination of a perfect home and where?


My imagination of a perfect home is a spacious apartment in an old building with large windows, squeaky wooden floors and high ceilings. The walls are full of pictures and paintings. There are plants everywhere and cute little reminders of all the countries I've been to. The apartment is located in Cologne with an amazing view of the Rhine.
