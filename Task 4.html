"I saw, I share": Select one of the other groups' medleys and reflect upon it?

Task 4

Jakob was playing Bennet's older brother and the two of them were talking about their plans for the future. Jakob as Bennet's older brother seemed to have it all figured out while Bennet on the other hand was very insecure of his plans for the future. For the next part of the medley Bennet and Leyla were on stage. Leyla told Bennet about her big plans for her future. She also told him how she was very insecure about telling her parents because she was unsure if her parents would support her plans.

In my opinion both parts of the medley were highly relatable because especially at our age, it is difficult for most people to decide on a path. On the one hand because of the general uncertainty in these difficult times, but above all out of fear of little support from parents, as in Leyla's example.
