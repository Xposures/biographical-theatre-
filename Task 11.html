General reflection: What were your experiences when working with the approach of biographical theatre? What did you learn from working with your own biography?

Task 11

I had a lot of fun working with my own biography and biographical theater and I also learned a couple of things about myself and my classmates. I also think it was great that everyone was able to develop their own scene for this topic. This allowed me to be much more creative and bring my own biography to the stage exactly as I imagined it. I was also able to gain a lot of clarity about myself and my life through the intensive examination of myself and my biography. The many tasks about our pasts in particular reminded me of what a mostly beautiful childhood I had, but also helped me to look positively into the future.
