Anecdotes: Choose two more terms
and write down the anecdotes connected to them?

Task 1

Writing

My childhood, my teenage years and my life overall can be described as somewhat challenging. With my parents divorce the COVID pandemic and other difficult situations.
Until two years ago I had no good way to deal with these kinds of situations. That was until I started writing.
I bought a small black journal and started writing in it whenever something happened in my life. No matter how small it seemed.
This helped me a lot throughout the years and that is why writing is such an important aspect of my life.

To this day I wrote several short stories and poems.


My own room

The privilege to have an own room is something I am extremely grateful for. Because ever since I was little my room was my safe space. A place where I could be myself. A place where I could do whatever I want and a place where thousands of great memories were forged. It never mattered where my room was located, at my Dad's or my Mom's. I always felt safe in my room.


My sister

My sister and I always had a very close relationship. This may be because of our similar interests and our shared political engagement. I can always talk to them and they can always talk to me. So sometimes we just sit in my room and talk about what happend in our lives and I really value these conversations because I don't have to hide anything unlike with other people. Sometimes we even have telepathic conversations.
