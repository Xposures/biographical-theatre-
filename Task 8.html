my postcard: Insert a picture of your postcard and present a short reflection on why it is biographically important to you/ why you were able to connect to it?

Task 8

I chose this postcard because I see the individuality of every person as our greatest good. I have always fallen out of what society calls "normal". I was never a boy with short hair who liked to play soccer in his free time and it took me a long time to accept that. To accept that I am not "normal" in the eyes of society. But once I accepted that, I realized how wonderful it is not to be "normal". How beautiful it is to see and experience the diversity of humanity. How beautiful each person is in their own way. The "normal" ones lost themselves and their individuality when they adapted to fit in with the other seemingly "normal" ones. That's why I chose this postcard, because I find these "normal" people who have lost themselves quite creepy.
