Task 6

The scene opens to an almost empty stage. On the stage are only multiple white signs with black text on them hanging from the ceiling and me. The lights are bright white and there is uncomfortable noise in the background. Written onto the signs are multiple negative thoughts memories etc. I go through the signs, pick one of them from the ceiling and explain what the sign is about. Then I crumple up the paper and throw it away. Then I go on to the next Sign and do the same all over again.

This scene shows how negative thoughts and situations can affect a persons life and how fighting against these thoughts is Sisyphus work. Through the lights and the noise an uncomfortable atmosphere is created which further shows the far reaching consequences of negative thoughts.
